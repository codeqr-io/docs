With `@codeqr/analytics`, you can track lead and sale conversions on your website, enabling you to measure the effectiveness of your marketing campaigns.

You can add the `@codeqr/analytics` script to your website same way you would add Google Analytics script or any other JavaScript code – by adding the `@codeqr/analytics` script in the `<head>` section of your HTML file.

```html
<script src="https://codeqr-cdn.pages.dev/analytics/script.js" defer />
```
