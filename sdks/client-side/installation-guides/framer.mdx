With `@codeqr/analytics`, you can track lead and sale conversions on your Framer site, enabling you to measure the effectiveness of your marketing campaigns.

You can add the `@codeqr/analytics` script to your Framer website same way you would add Google Analytics script or any other JavaScript code.

Follow these steps to add the script to your site:

- Go to your Framer project and open the **Project Settings** menu.
- Open the **General** tab and scroll down to the **Custom Code** section.
- Paste the CodeQR analytics script in the **Start of head tag** section.
- Click on the **Save** button to save the changes.

```html
<script src="https://codeqr-cdn.pages.dev/analytics/script.js" defer></script>
```
